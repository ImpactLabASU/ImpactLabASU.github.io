Welcome to the Workshop

Join us for an in-depth exploration of ethical challenges in multi-modal AI for healthcare. This workshop addresses critical issues such as AI's role in precision medicine, expert-guided decision making, and the ethical considerations of AI-driven diagnostics.

Key Themes

  • Explainability and transparency in multi-modal AI
  • Human-AI collaboration in medical decision-making
  • Patient-centric AI approaches for personalized care
  • Ethical use of AI in high-stakes medical applications

Explore the links above for more details on the organizing committee, paper submission guidelines, key dates, and the program schedule. We look forward to your contributions and participation in shaping the future of ethical AI in healthcare.
