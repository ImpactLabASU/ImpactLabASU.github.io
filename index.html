Welcome to the Workshop

The workshop focuses on the ethical development and evaluation of multi-modal AI in critical healthcare applications, such as precision medicine, surgical planning, and disease diagnostics. Join experts in the field as they explore cutting-edge research on AI explainability, ethics, and collaboration with clinicians to ensure safe, reliable, and transparent medical AI systems.
